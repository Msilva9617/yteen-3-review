Y-Teen 3 Final Exam

Interactive Revision Quiz

Question 1
Choose the correct option to talk about Julia's last vacation: "Last year, Julia ______ to an amazing ecotourism experience in Mato Grosso."
Hint: Remember that we are describing a trip that already happened ("Last year") and the verb is irregular.
Question 2
Which sentence correctly compares the food prices between the two places from the text? "The food in Mato Grosso is ______ than the food in São Paulo."
Hint: Short adjectives (one syllable) receive a structural change directly at the end of the word to indicate comparison.
Question 3
Doug is talking about his green plans to protect the environment next week. Complete his statement: "I ______ shorter showers to save water."
Hint: To talk about future plans with the personal pronoun 'I', you need the auxiliary verb 'am' before 'going to'.
Question 4
Esther wants to give some eco-friendly advice to Doug. What is the most appropriate phrase? "Doug, you ______ aerosol sprays. It's bad for the planet."
Hint: Identify the ideal modal verb to express direct advice that something should not be done because it is a bad idea.
Question 5
Ginny is talking about a past choice from last Saturday. Choose the correct pattern: "My family wanted to go to a restaurant, but I ______ to stay home and order pizza."
Hint: The sentence contextualizes a decision made in the past ('wanted'). Apply the Simple Past form of the verb 'prefer'.
Question 6
Tom is suggesting a fun option to his friends for next weekend. Choose the correct pattern: "How about ______ a mystery board game?"
Hint: Fixed expressions of suggestion like 'How about...?' modify the format of the next verb, adding three letters to its end.
